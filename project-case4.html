01 — Challenge

The Problem

The marketing team had no reliable way to demonstrate ROI on campaign spend or identify which customer segments were actually generating profitable revenue. Key challenges included:

  • Unclear return on marketing investment across channels and campaigns
  • Inefficient targeting — budgets spread broadly rather than focused on value segments
  • Poor attribution of conversions and channel contribution to actual revenue
  • No reliable method to identify campaign winners vs. underperformers quickly
  • Limited visibility into customer lifetime value and long-term behaviour patterns

Core issue: Without a unified analytics view, leadership was making multi-million dollar investment decisions based on partial data and gut feel — rather than evidence.

02 — Approach

My Role & Approach

I built a comprehensive analytics framework and Power BI dashboard that transformed raw campaign data into actionable marketing intelligence — connecting spend, behaviour, and revenue in one unified view.

a. Data Preparation

  • Integrated campaign, customer, sales, and channel datasets from multiple sources
  • Standardised KPI definitions for spend, conversions, and attributed revenue
  • Created calculated metrics for CAC, LTV, conversion rate, and campaign ROI

b. Exploratory Analysis

  • Identified high-value customer segments and mapped their behavioural performance drivers
  • Compared campaign effectiveness across channels, segments, and budget levels
  • Analysed funnel drop-off points to locate conversion gaps and wasted spend

c. Dashboard Development

  • Executive KPI Cards — ROI, CAC, total revenue, and conversion rates at a glance
  • Attribution Analysis — channel and touchpoint contribution to each conversion
  • Funnel & Conversion Tracking — stage-by-stage visibility into campaign performance
  • Segment Performance Views — linking customer behaviour directly to revenue impact
  • Budget Scenario Tool — modelling expected revenue from spend reallocation options
03 — Insights

Key Insights Uncovered

  1. Top-spending campaigns were not the most profitable. Several high-spend channels generated strong volume but low ROI — budget was flowing to channels that looked good on surface metrics but underdelivered on margin.
  2. High-value segments were identifiable by behaviour, not reach. The most profitable customers could be isolated using purchase behaviour signals — enabling far more precise targeting than the existing demographic-only approach.
  3. Attribution gaps were hiding underperforming spend. Better multi-touch tracking revealed entire channels that contributed little incremental value — significant budget was being allocated based on flawed last-click logic.
  4. Mid-funnel drop-offs were the biggest conversion killer. The analysis pinpointed where prospects were disengaging — optimising these specific stages delivered measurable conversion gains without increasing spend.
  5. Budget reallocation was a fast path to growth. Redirecting spend from low-ROI channels to top-performing segments projected higher expected revenue with a materially lower CAC.
04 — Impact

Solution Impact

  • Provided a single, reliable source of truth for all campaign performance data
  • Enabled marketing leadership to prioritise and double down on high-value segments
  • Reduced wasted spend by identifying and cutting low-performing channels
  • Improved cross-functional collaboration between analytics, marketing, and finance
  • Turned campaign data into a repeatable, scalable decision-making process
