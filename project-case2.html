Retail Performance Dashboard

Leveraging Data Analytics to Optimize Pricing and Discount Strategies

The Problem

The retail business was operating with limited visibility into how discount strategies impacted profitability and customer behavior. Key challenges included:

  • Black Box Discounting: No clear understanding of which products benefited from discounts and which didn't
  • Margin Erosion: Excessive discounting on products that could have sold at full price
  • Decision Making: Pricing decisions were based on intuition rather than data-driven insights
  • Competitive Analysis: No mechanism to compare performance against benchmarks
  • Reporting Delays: Manual Excel reports took 2-3 days to compile and were error-prone

My Solution

Data Architecture & Modeling

I designed a comprehensive data model that brought together sales, inventory, and financial data:

  • Built star schema combining sales transactions, product master, and calendar dimensions
  • Created calculated fields for margin analysis and profitability metrics
  • Established data quality rules and validation checks

Dashboard Development

Developed interactive Power BI dashboards with multiple analytical layers:

  • Executive Overview: High-level KPIs showing revenue, margin, and trend performance
  • Product Analysis: Deep dive into product-level performance including discount impact correlation
  • Discount Effectiveness: Analysis showing ROI of discount strategies by product category
  • Comparative Analysis: Benchmarking products within categories and against previous periods
  • Pricing Recommendations: ML-enhanced suggestions for optimal pricing

Advanced Analytics

  • Implemented regression analysis to quantify discount elasticity by product
  • Created price optimization models using historical data
  • Built predictive models for demand forecasting
