Business intelligence

I am a Business Intelligence Analyst with over four years of experience translating complex data into clear, actionable insights. My background spans financial analysis, customer behaviour analytics, competitive monitoring, and BI dashboard development.

I am currently transitioning into the Swedish market and completing an MSc in Business Intelligence at Dalarna University. I bring both the technical depth and the stakeholder communication skills to make data work for the whole organisation — not just the data team.

What drives me is the intersection of analytics, technology, and real business impact. I do not build dashboards for dashboards' sake. I build tools that help people make faster, smarter, more confident decisions.
