Healthcare Case Management System

Streamlining Patient Care Coordination and Compliance in a Multi-Facility Healthcare Network

View Live Dashboard Project Details

The Problem

A large healthcare network with 12 facilities was struggling with fragmented patient care coordination and compliance tracking. The lack of a unified case management system created significant operational and regulatory challenges:

  • Patient Care Fragmentation: Patient information was scattered across multiple EMR systems with no unified view of care history
  • Care Coordination Gaps: Multiple care teams worked in silos, leading to duplicated services and missed follow-ups
  • Compliance Risks: Difficulty tracking regulatory requirements (HIPAA, Joint Commission standards) across facilities
  • Resource Inefficiency: Staff spent excessive time searching for patient information and coordinating care manually
  • Quality Metrics Blind Spots: No centralized tracking of patient outcomes, readmission rates, or care quality indicators
  • Financial Impact: Inefficient care coordination led to increased length of stay and reduced reimbursement rates
  • Patient Safety Concerns: Medication reconciliation and care transition issues due to information gaps

My Solution

Comprehensive Stakeholder Analysis & Requirements Gathering

I led a 3-month discovery phase involving all key stakeholders across the healthcare network:

  • Conducted 45+ interviews with physicians, nurses, case managers, administrators, and IT staff
  • Facilitated 12 cross-functional workshops to map current state workflows and identify pain points
  • Analyzed regulatory requirements (HIPAA, Joint Commission, CMS guidelines) and compliance needs
  • Documented 200+ functional and non-functional requirements using user stories and acceptance criteria
  • Created detailed process flows for patient admission, care coordination, discharge planning, and follow-up care

System Architecture & Design

Designed a comprehensive case management solution integrating with existing EMR systems:

  • Patient-Centric Case Model: Created unified patient profiles with care team assignments, care plans, and progress tracking
  • Care Coordination Workflows: Automated care transitions, handoffs, and follow-up scheduling with escalation protocols
  • Compliance Automation: Built-in regulatory compliance tracking, audit trails, and automated reporting for Joint Commission standards
  • Real-Time Dashboards: Executive dashboards for quality metrics, utilization rates, and patient outcomes
  • Mobile Access: Secure mobile application for care team members to access patient information and update care plans
  • Integration Layer: APIs connecting to Epic EMR, lab systems, pharmacy systems, and external payer platforms

Implementation & Change Management

Led the 9-month implementation across all 12 facilities with comprehensive training and support:

  • Developed detailed implementation roadmap with phased rollout strategy
  • Created comprehensive training materials and conducted 25 training sessions for 300+ users
  • Established super-user program with 12 facility champions for ongoing support
  • Implemented change management strategy with regular communication and feedback loops
  • Set up help desk and support processes for post-go-live assistance
