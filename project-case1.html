Retail Performance Dashboard

Transforming pricing, discount visibility, and margin analytics for retail stakeholders.

Watch Retail Dashboard Video Download Case Study PDF

The Problem

Retail leadership lacked a single source of truth for discount effectiveness and category profitability. This created challenges across pricing, inventory, and promotional planning:

  • Unclear Discount Impact: No clear signal of which promotions improved revenue vs. eroded margin.
  • Inventory Pressure: Overstock and clearance risk due to misaligned pricing decisions.
  • Fragmented Reporting: Manual reports from multiple systems delayed decisions by days.
  • Limited Category Insights: The business could not reliably compare product category performance.
  • Reactive Pricing: Pricing decisions were driven by intuition rather than data.

My Solution

Integrated Data Model

I built an end-to-end retail analytics model that combined sales, inventory, product, and discount data in a single view.

  • Created a star schema with sales transactions, product hierarchy, pricing, and calendar dimensions.
  • Brought together discounted and non-discounted revenue streams for clear margin comparison.
  • Added calculated metrics for gross profit, discount lift, and price elasticity.
  • Validated data quality through sales reconciliation and inventory consistency checks.

Interactive Dashboard Experience

Developed a consumer-facing Power BI dashboard that connected executive metrics with tactical insights.

  • Executive KPIs: Revenue, margin, discount effectiveness, and trend performance.
  • Category Performance: Compare product lines and identify underperforming promotions.
  • Discount Analysis: Evaluate the profitability of every promotion by SKU and category.
  • Price Optimization: Visualize recommended pricing adjustments against historical demand.
  • Real-Time Insight: Embedded refresh logic to keep analytics aligned with daily inventory updates.

Advanced Retail Analytics

  • Built trend-based forecasts to estimate demand response to pricing changes.
  • Implemented ROI measurements for promotional spend and margin impact.
  • Created competitor-style benchmark views to highlight pricing gaps.

Key Outcomes:

  • Delivered improved pricing decisions that supported higher margin product mix.
  • Enabled merchandising teams to identify and remove low-ROI promotions.

Recommendations & Next Steps

Short-term Enhancements (0-3 months):

  • Expand the dashboard to include customer segment profitability.
  • Introduce alerting for below-target margin and clearance risk.
  • Implement a pricing feedback loop for promotions and markdowns.

Medium-term Growth (3-6 months):

  • Build dynamic pricing scenarios based on elasticity and inventory levels.
  • Integrate competitor price intelligence for category benchmarking.
  • Incorporate customer basket analysis to support cross-sell decisions.

Long-term Strategy (6+ months):

  • Deploy machine-learning models to recommend optimal discount levels.
  • Roll out mobile dashboards for store managers and field analysts.
  • Establish automated pricing governance and approval workflows.

This retail performance dashboard replaced fragmented reporting with an integrated analytics experience, empowering the business to make data-driven pricing choices and protect margin.
